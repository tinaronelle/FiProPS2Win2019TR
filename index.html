Harlem is Home

Harlem Enchanted is a homage to the beauty behind the bricks in the village of Harlem, New York City. Here we feature the interiors, art and design inspiration of ordinary and extraordinary Harlem residents. Some interesting choices and solutions arise from lots of space or limited space. I am always inspired and hope that you are too. There are some interesting people in this neighborhood, so I have chosen not only to showcase their apartments, but also their personalities.
