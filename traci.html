Traci's Serene Space

Ohm is Where the Heart Is

I am not sure if I have a design style but my space is a combination of antiques and some modern pieces. I love to hang things on the wall but I do not have a lot of design space.



I am totally a minimalist and am always looking to get rid of something. Whenever I bring in new furniture I give a piece of furniture away. I love my apartment but I want to be able to move easily if I choose to. I also collect design magazines so you will see those stacked all around my apartment.



For my favorite room is my bedroom because that is where I spend the most time. I have chosen colors that make me feel relaxed and I like it to be as minimal as possible. My bedroom windows face a garden and I keep my shades open all day because that adds to the serenity. Second runner up for favorite room is my living room, which I never ever use, but it is pretty!
