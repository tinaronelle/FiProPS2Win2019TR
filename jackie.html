Jackie Allan's Eclectic Pad

Jackie Allan's Eclectic Pad

I live in a walk up building on the top floor in the last apartment, so it is pretty quiet. I have two bedrooms, one I use for a spare den and sometimes bedroom. There is one big bathroom which is not at all modern but I do not mind because there is a lot of space. I used to entertain a lot but my schedule is so busy lately I have not had time to do much of that lately.



I like street art so I have a lot of unknown artists hanging on the wall. Also I have a lot of pictures of my family which is really important to me. I am into purples and lavenders, so my living room walls are that color.



My favorite room would have to be my living room. It is where I spend the majority of my time (when I am home) catching up on Netflix or listening to Jazz, which is my favorite type of music. I have an amazing couch, which is so comfy and large that I do almost everything on it. I study, read, sometimes nap and sometimes eat on it. My living room windows are where I get the most light so that is another reason I spend a lot of time there. Also, I love my plants and they are in my living room as well.
